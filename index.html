FAQ

Frequently asked questions about screening, search, and payroll outsourcing in Vietnam.

These supporting questions help both buyers and search engines understand the service intent behind the page.

What does a background check in Vietnam usually include?

A standard scope may include identity verification, employment history checks, education verification, reference checks, and where applicable, criminal-record coordination. The final scope should match the role risk level and your hiring policy.

When should a company use executive search in Vietnam instead of general recruitment?

Executive search is better suited to confidential mandates, leadership hires, specialist positions, and cases where direct outreach to passive talent is necessary. It adds process control and stakeholder discipline to higher-value searches.

What does Vietnam payroll outsourcing help solve?

Payroll outsourcing helps employers reduce recurring administrative load, improve reporting consistency, and operate with more confidence around local payroll coordination requirements.
